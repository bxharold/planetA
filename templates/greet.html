Earthquake Sequencer:

Displays an animation of locations, magnitudes, and time intervals of a set of earthquakes.
The UI presents a pulldown list of all available tables that were created by the earthquake data aggregator app, 'dbquakes4planet.py'.

ISS Tracker:

This displays the location of the International Space Station in real time, with an update every 5 seconds.
Also displays the name of the state, country or ocean that ISS is currently over.
In non-Guest (dev) mode, ISS Tracker offers the option of displaying a recorded path.
